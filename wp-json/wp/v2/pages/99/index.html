Over the past years the college has developed an extremely well equipped laboratory base for imparting experimental and practical knowledge in Science faculty. A science laboratory is functioning in the college.
The laboratory is equipped with various apparatus for conducting experiments in Physics\/Chemistry\/Biology that are appropriate for all students. The library is established with the foundation of college. It has a\u00a0large collection of books, journals and non-book materials. In addition\u00a0to the books from main library, some more books are issued to students\u00a0from book bank also. To the students belonging to weaker section (SC\/ST)\u00a0some additional books are given from the Book Bank. Xeroxing facility\u00a0is available to the users at very nominal rate.\u00a0<\/p>

There is a video viewing facility. Large number of books, periodicals, video cassettes and CD-ROMs are available. The library is being fully computerized. The CDNET facilities are to be developed very soon. It caters to the needs of large groups of users including more than 2000 students, faculties, and equally large number of supporting staff.<\/p>

It has computerized all its house-keeping activities using global software that is being maintained and updated regularly. It also facilitates industries, individual consultants and corporates to access online database and journals. It uses state-of-the-art technology in its functioning and services. Not only having a very good reference section, Library has been subscribed to access all AICTE approved journals and magazines. Following services are also being provided to the users.<\/p>
